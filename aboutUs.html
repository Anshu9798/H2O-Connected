Re-envisioning water conservation in a new home.

H2O Connected, LLC was founded by the divestiture of nth Solutions, LLC’s water technology division into a separate, woman owned QOZ Business located in Coatesville PA. The Company’s legacy team of professionals continue to manufacture and support all their water-saving and water-damage prevention products including the multi-patented LeakAlertor 6000® and the LeakAlertor® Pro System. Additionally, H2O Connected continues to develop innovative, Made in the USA, products for the Multi-Family, Hospitality, and Consumer markets. All of our products are designed to promote wise water stewardship and cost-effective management of this precious natural resource.

H20 Connected is currently renovating a historic building in downtown Coatesville into what will soon be its new office and manufacturing facility.

Where it all started...

nth Solutions, LLC is a vertically integrated product development, manufacturing, and business incubation company. Its design team holds more than 50 commercialized patents. Founded by Eric L Canfield and Susan L Springsteen in 2006, the Company “Advances Innovation From Concept to Commercialization” with a focus on products that “Save Lives, Save Money, and Preserve Natural Resources”. Currently located in Exton PA, nth Solutions will soon join H2O Connected in its new headquarters in Coatesville. This exciting incubation ecosystem will be known as the nth Innovation Center.
