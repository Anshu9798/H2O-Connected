Where it all started...

Before H20 Connected, the LeakAlertor products were developed by nth Solutions, a vertically integrated product development & manufacturing company whose design team holds more than 50 commercialized patents. Founded by Eric Canfield and Susan Springsteen in 2006, the company has lived by its mission to Save Money, Save Lives, and Preserve Natural Resources for over a decade. Currently based in Exton, Pennsylvania, nth Solutions involves a whole team of interns, marketers, and engineers to develop and expand intellectual property for commercialization, distribution, and sales. The company continues today to develop a wide range of unique products for homes and businesses!
